Welcome to the Homestuck Comic Maker!

A simple canvas app (works best on PC screens)intended to create panel images similar to the ones in Homestuck.

Instructions

  1. Click the sprites to add them
  2. Hold alt(⊞ Win)/cmd⌘ (mac) whilst clicking to select multiple sprites on the canvas
  3. Hotkeys: CTRL+Z (undo), CTRL+Y (redo), and DEL (delete)
  4. Move the canvas background with the arrow keys
