Welcome to the Homestuck Comic Maker!

This is a pretty simple canvas app (works best on pc screens), and is intended to create panel images similar to the ones in Homestuck.

Version: 1.0.0

Instructions

  1. Add images by clicking on the buttons in the tabbed menu
  2. You can edit, rezize, and rotate sprites
  3. You can select multiple items by holding alt while clicking on them
  4. Hotkeys include: CTRL+Z (undo), CTRL+Y (redo), and DEL (delete)
  5. The exported image's background is transparent so you can customise it.
